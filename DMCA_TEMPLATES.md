## Template 1: Standard Takedown Notice

**Subject:** DMCA Takedown Notice — Unauthorized Use of Copyrighted Work

Dear {platform_name} Legal Team,

I, {rights_owner}, am the owner (or authorized agent) of copyrighted work titled **{original_work_title}** located at:
- Original URL: {original_url}

I have identified infringing material at:
- Infringing URL(s): {infringing_url}

I have a good-faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notice is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner.

Please remove or disable access to the infringing material promptly.

Contact information:
- Name: {contact_name}
- Company (optional): {company_name}
- Email: {contact_email}
- Phone: {contact_phone}
- Address: {contact_address}

Electronic signature:
{electronic_signature}

Date: {notice_date}
Case reference (optional): {case_reference}

中文补充：
- 适用于常规侵权下架申请。
- 需确认原作品链接、侵权链接、权利主体信息完整。

## Template 2: Urgent Escalation Notice

**Subject:** URGENT DMCA Escalation — Ongoing Harm / Rapid Reposting

Dear {platform_name} Trust & Safety / Legal,

This is an urgent escalation regarding repeated unauthorized distribution of copyrighted material owned by {rights_owner}.

Primary original work:
- {original_work_title}
- {original_url}

Active infringing links (rapid spread observed):
- {infringing_url}
- {additional_infringing_urls}

Urgency rationale:
- {harm_summary}
- {time_sensitivity_reason}

Prior report references (if any):
- {prior_ticket_ids}

I confirm in good faith that this use is unauthorized, and under penalty of perjury, I affirm this notice is accurate and that I am authorized to act.

Please prioritize immediate removal, hash matching, and re-upload suppression where available.

Contact:
- {contact_name} / {contact_email} / {contact_phone}

Electronic signature:
{electronic_signature}
Date: {notice_date}

中文补充：
- 适用于持续扩散、短时高风险场景。
- 建议附上已提交工单号与扩散证据。
